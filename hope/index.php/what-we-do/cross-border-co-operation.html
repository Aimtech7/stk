Cross-border Co-operation

Cross-border Peace & Security

In an effort to contribute to the region’s peace and security, we work to ensure;

  1. Strengthen cross border cohesion and improve natural resource management in the borderlands
  • Secure cross-border mobility as  a resilience practice of pastoralist communities 
  • Strengthen cross border Advocacy 
  1. Strengthen & integrate peacebuilding approaches in security governance.
  • Advocate for a human rights lens in peace and security policy processes. 
  1. Enhance community capacities  for meaningful participation in peace processes
  • Amplifying voice of excluded populations (women, youth,PWDs) in peace processes.
  • Advance community participatory research to inform evidence based engagements.
