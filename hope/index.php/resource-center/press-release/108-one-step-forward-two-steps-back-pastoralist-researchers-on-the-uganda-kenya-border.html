One step forward, two steps back: pastoralist researchers on the Uganda / Kenya border

Hits: 799
Share:
To preserve, promote, and celebrate cultural heritage and the environment by empowering people of all ages to engage, learn, and pass on traditions, ensuring a thriving cultural legacy for future generations. A unified, informed, dignified, and prosperous community. Core Objectives: Advocate for community interests, culture, and language. Promote socio-economic development. Support sustainable environmental management. Defend and preserve cultural heritage. Focus Areas: Cultural preservation and promotion. Community empowerment and capacity building. Environmental conservation. Cultural education and awareness. Impact: Positive social and economic transformation within communities. Improved livelihoods and well-being. Increased cultural awareness and participation. Values: Authenticity. Inclusivity. Sustainability. Stewardship. Get involved, partner with us, volunteer, or support our programs to preserve cultural heritage and empower communities.
