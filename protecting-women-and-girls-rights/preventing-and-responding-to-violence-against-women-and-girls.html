Stop! Killing Women

HeForShe is an invitation for men and people of all genders to stand in solidarity with women to create a bold, visible and united force for gender equality.

Stop Killing women. Let's end femicide!

Women in Kenya are sick of the increase in violence against them. Over 500 women were killed in Kenya from 2016 to 2023, according to Africa Data Hub. Three-quarters of the killings were at the hands of a person the victim knew. At least 10 Kenyan women were victims of femicide in January alone.
